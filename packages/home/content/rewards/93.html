Preparing for Nakamoto Release (3.8% reserve)

This year, the Nakamoto hard fork will bring sBTC. This means that we will wrap BTC to sBTC, then exchange to STX and distribute.

However, stackers need to do more work eventually, run stacks and a signer node. We successfully started to be signer for Stacks 2.5 and are registered since cycle #84 as signer. We completed the first cycle handover between cycle #92 and #93.

For cycle #93, we transferred 2,209.286692 STX (3%) and 0.09407508 BTC from STX distribution and 0.06340796 BTC from BTC distribution to the reserve (see above).That is 5349,288043 STX or around 3.8% of the theoretical total STX rewards after converting BTC to STX at the exchange rate of 2944 sats/stx (4,52,243,607 sats / 153,601.284823 STX).

Swapping using Simpleswap

We received 0.02851755 BTC cashback from simpleswap for using their service that we transferred to the Fast Pool reserve. However, this amount was part of the balance to calculate the rewards during swapping after the second consolidation. Therefore, the fees are lower.

One swap request was rejected by Simpleswaps and then refunded. This happened shortly before the deadline for reward distribution that is implemented for Lisa. Therefore, we swapped the missing amount of 0.32999534 BTC using 11,427.021048 STX from the Fast Pool reserve at the rate of 2887. This corresponded to the exchange rate of coingecko at the time.
