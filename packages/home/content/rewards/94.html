Total Rewards

A total of 132,524.986548 STX were distributed to 729 pool members and 5 contracts. The distribution was based on 40,692,934.629964 STX stacked to receive STX rewards.

A total of 1.05620493 BTC were distributed to 1 pool member from the proxy btc address. The distribution was based on 11,482,001 STX stacked to receive BTC rewards.

If all BTC rewards were distributed as STX rewards the total would have been 169,918.506114 STX based on 52,174,935.629964 STX stacked.

Preparing for Nakamoto Release (4.3% reserve)

This year, the Nakamoto hard fork will bring sBTC. This means that we will wrap BTC to sBTC, then exchange to STX and distribute.

However, stackers need to do more work eventually, run stacks and a signer node. We successfully started to be signer for Stacks 2.5 and are registered since cycle #84 as signer. We completed the first cycle handover between cycle #92 and #93.

For cycle #94, we transferred 2,802.931684 STX and 2,718.942802 STX from STX distribution and 0.05550393 BTC from BTC distribution to the reserve (see above). That is 7.388,807886 STX or around 4.3% of the theoretical total STX rewards after converting BTC to STX at the exchange rate of 2973 sats/stx (3,94,027,032 sats / 132,524.986548 STX).
